Pasta


Description

This homemade pasta recipe is great and easy to make with flour,eggs,salts, olive oil, and water. Roll out by hand or use a pasta machine to cut dough into desired pasta shape.

In this recipe you will find the ingredient lists and step by step instruction in the recipe below!

Ingredients

Now let's talk about the ingredients needed to create this pasta.

  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons water,or as needed
