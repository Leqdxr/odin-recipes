Basic Burger


Description

This hamburger patty recipe uses ground beef and an easy bread crumb mixture! Nothing beats a simple hamburger on a warm summer evening! Enjoy on ciabatta, Kaiser, or potato rolls topped with your favourite condiments.

You can make this burger at home with minimal ingredients. The expected time taken to create this masterpiece is around 15 minutes only! So it is definitely worth giving a try.

Ingredients

Now lets get started with the ingredients needed to make this delicious burger!

The ingredients are:

  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

Steps

Only 5 ingredients just as promised. Now let's get started with actually creating this thing. It consists of total of 6 steps and they all should be follwed in order.

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high and lightly oil grate.
  2. Whisk egg,salt and pepper together in a medium bowl.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
  4. Form into four 3/4 inch thick patties.
  5. Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!

That was easy, if you followed the steps correctly, you will have a burger ready to be devoured right in front of you!
